Our Story

Born from a
passion for scent.

Christian's Designer Scents was founded on a single belief: that fragrance is one of the most personal forms of self-expression. Every drop we craft is an invitation to feel something extraordinary.

We source rare botanicals from across the world — rose absolute from Grasse, oud resin from the Middle East, cedarwood from the Atlas Mountains — and combine them with meticulous artistry.

Our Craft

The art of
perfumery

From raw material to finished bottle, every step is deliberate.

01

Sourcing

We travel the world to find the finest raw ingredients — no compromises on quality or provenance.

02

Composition

Our perfumers layer top, heart, and base notes with precision, balancing each accord by hand.

03

Maceration

Blends rest for weeks, allowing ingredients to fully harmonize into a seamless, complex fragrance.

04

Bottling

Each bottle is filled, sealed, and finished by hand — then inspected before it reaches you.
